Returns Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you are not happy for any reason, we accept returns within 30 days of delivery.

How to Return

  1. Email us at returns@localtesttim.com with your order number
  2. We will send you a prepaid return label within 24 hours
  3. Pack the item securely and drop it off at any post office
  4. Refund is processed within 3-5 business days of receiving the item

Conditions

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Sale items are final sale and cannot be returned
  • Damaged or defective items are replaced at no charge
